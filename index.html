      Dashboard Help & Features

      What is the Productivity Dashboard?

      This app is designed for anyone who wants to stay organized and get things done. Whether you're managing work projects, personal goals, side businesses, or exploring new ideas, this dashboard helps you keep track of everything without feeling overwhelmed.

      The key insight: Not all tasks are the same. Some things need your attention right now, while others are ideas for the future. This app separates your active tasks from your future ideas so you can focus on what matters today.

      How It Works: The Two-Track System

      The dashboard uses a simple but powerful concept:

      • Execution Track: Projects and tasks you're actively working on right now
      • Incubation Track: Ideas, future projects, and things to explore later

      This prevents great ideas from cluttering your daily workflow while ensuring they don't get forgotten.

      Getting Started: Your First Steps

      1
      Create Themes: Click "Aspects" to create categories like Work, Personal, Side Projects, etc.
      2
      Add Projects: Use "New Project" to create your first project in any theme
      3
      Choose Status: Mark each project as "Execution" (active) or "Incubation" (future)
      4
      Add Tasks: Break projects into actionable tasks with due dates and priorities
      5
      Start Focusing: Use Focus Mode to concentrate on what's due and important

      The Secret Sauce: Focus Mode

      This is where the magic happens. Focus Mode shows you:

      • Overdue Tasks: What needs immediate attention
      • Due Today: Your daily commitments
      • Execution Board: All your active project tasks

      The focus timer continues running even when you switch apps - perfect for writing, planning, or any deep work that requires jumping between tools.

      Three Ways to View Your Work

      • Project View: See all projects organized by themes (Work, Personal, etc.) - great for planning
      • Board View: Kanban-style cards showing Execution vs Incubation - perfect for quick triage
      • Focus Mode: Distraction-free view of what needs your attention today - ideal for deep work

      Track Your Progress

      The dashboard shows you patterns in your work:

      • Daily Stats: Tasks completed, time focused, productivity streaks
      • Monthly Heatmaps: Visualize when you're most active with ideas and execution
      • Goals: Set long-term objectives and track progress

      This helps you understand your natural rhythms and optimize accordingly.

      Your Data Stays Private

      Everything is stored locally on your computer. No internet required, no data shared, complete privacy. Your productivity insights are yours alone.
